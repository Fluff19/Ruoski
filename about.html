The Country of the Ruosk Islands is located near The Royal Manor of Portland, Dorsetshire, The United Kingdom. Our motto is "Fi a’m nerth", in English it translates to "I and my strength". Our offical languages are: Welsh, Cornish, and British English, and our capital is Urêura on the island of Albatross which is in the Northern Region. Our currency is the shilling (which is estimated to be 20p). Our calling code is +44.
